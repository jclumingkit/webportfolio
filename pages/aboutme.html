About Me

Bio

In my professional career, I'm what you call a generalist. I've worked in different business sectors and industries. Some may say being a generalist may hinder future career progressions. I disagree in the sense that working in different fields allowed me to gather a variety of skills that will prove to be helpful in my future career as a web developer.

Work Experience

Before transitioning to freelance work, I started working in a BPO company as a call center agent. When an opportunity came to work as a freelance admin assistant, I grabbed it once I was sure it would be a long term work.

Then six months later, I learned how to sell my skills through copywriting. A couple of months after, I was hired as a social media manager. The job was to create engaging content in Instagram.

In December 2021, a client reached out in LinkedIn saying she wanted to work with me. It turns out she saw my work from a previous client. She liked how it was so organized.

At the time of this writing, I have three clients, and I work as an admin assistant, social media manager, and graphic designer, respectively.

Now, I'm focused on learning web development and eventually land a job as a front-end developer or a product designer.
